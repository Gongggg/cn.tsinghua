IG Scope:

The Medical Multimedia Interest Group (MMIG) of the IEEE Technical Committee on Multimedia Communications promotes research and development in the emerging areas of processing and analysis of multimedia data (videos, CT/MR images, medical examination reports, medical records, etc.) for medical purposes, such as medical image understanding, computer aided diagnosis, medical data communication and tele-medicine.
MMIG aims to contribute to future developments in medical multimedia processing and analysis by providing a platform for researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to discuss relevant topics and exchange views on the important issues of medical multimedia. It will assist the IEEE MMTC by organizing and supporting ComSoc-sponsored conferences and workshops, and special issues of relevant journals. The technical topics of MMIG will span, but not limited to the following:
  • Medical multimedia content management for communication and collaboration
  • Medical image/video registration, segmentation and detection
  • Medical multimedia indexing and retrieval
  • Medical multimedia information analysis for diagnosis and prognosis
  • Medical multimedia visualization
  • Medical multimedia communication
  • Medical report generation from medical multimedia data
  • Visual data processing and analysis for other medical purposes
  • Security in medical multimedia data communication and processing
  • Other medical multimedia computing techniques
